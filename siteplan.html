Paws & Hearts Rescue - Website Plan

Site Name:

The name of the site will be "Paws & Hearts Rescue". This name reflects both the mission of the organization (rescuing dogs) and the emotional connection people have when adopting or supporting animals in need.

Optional Domain Availability: www.pawsandheartsrescue.org

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide information on adopting, fostering, and donating to a local dog rescue organization. It will feature adoptable dogs, the foster program, donation opportunities, upcoming events, and background information on the organization.

Scenarios:

1. A potential adopter is interested in seeing available dogs for adoption and learns about the adoption process.

2. A person wants to contribute to the rescue and is looking for donation options and details on how donations help the organization.

Color Scheme:

The color scheme for the site will be warm and inviting, representing both the rescue and the animals:

  • Primary Color: #FF6347 (Tomato) - Used for headings, buttons, and call-to-action elements.
  • Secondary Color: #4CAF50 (Green) - Used for backgrounds and accents to convey hope and nature.

Typography:

The primary font for headings will be "Playfair Display", which adds a touch of elegance and warmth. For body text, "Roboto" will be used for readability and clarity.
